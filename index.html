Start Your Own Branch

Welcome to the Start Your Own Branch page! We are so excited that you are interested in expanding the mission of Hopkinton Advocacy for the Unhoused to your own community. By starting a branch, you will be taking a direct stand against homelessness and helping to provide vital resources and support to those in need.

Why Start a Branch?

Starting a branch of HAU is a powerful way to make a difference. As a branch leader, you will:

  • Mobilize your community to support unhoused individuals.
  • Coordinate local donation drives and advocacy efforts.
  • Build a network of volunteers dedicated to social justice.
  • Receive mentorship and resources from the main HAU team.
