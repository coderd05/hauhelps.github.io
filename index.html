Start a Branch

We are always looking for additional branches in highschools. Please complete the steps outlined below to start your own branch journey.

Step 1.

Contact our Branch Coordinator, Maanya, at +1 508-395-0774. Let her know what grade you are in, what high school you go to, and that you intend to start a branch. She will help guide you through the club approval process and provide all necessary materials.

Step 2:

Contact Vice principal and express interest in starting club. You can use this template:

Subject: Interest in Starting a New Club

Hello Mr./Ms. [Vice Principal's Last Name],
I hope you're having a great day.

My name is [Your Name], and I'm a [grade level, e.g., freshman]. I've recently become involved with a nonprofit organization called Advocacy for the Unhoused, and I believe it would be a meaningful addition to our school community.

I'm interested in starting a branch of Advocacy for the Unhoused as a club here. Could you please let me know what the process is for starting a new club? Feel free to check out the organization's website at www.hauhelps.org for more information.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

Step 3:

  • Find a group of 5 people minimum to participate in the club.
  • Find a teacher sponsor/advisor. Since this is a student-run club, the teacher can work in any field.
Congrats. You are now an official branch of our non-profit.
