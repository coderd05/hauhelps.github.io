Start Your Own Branch

Welcome to the Start Your Own Branch page! We are so excited that you are interested in expanding the mission of Hopkinton Advocacy for the Unhoused to your own community. By starting a branch, you will be taking a direct stand against homelessness and helping to provide vital resources and support to those in need in your local area.

Why Start a Branch?

Starting a branch of HAU is a powerful way to make a difference. As a branch leader, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Mobilize your community to support unhoused individuals.
  • Coordinate local donation drives and advocacy efforts tailored to your community's specific needs.
  • Build a network of volunteers and supporters dedicated to social justice and housing equity.
  • Receive guidance, resources, and mentorship from the main HAU team.

What to Expect

Starting a branch is a significant commitment, but it's also incredibly rewarding. We'll work closely with you to ensure you have the tools and support you need to be successful. From initial setup to ongoing projects, the HAU team will be with you every step of the way.

Ready to Lead?

If you're ready to take the first step toward starting an HAU branch in your community, please click the button below to fill out our Branch Interest Form. Our team will review your application and reach out to you to discuss the next steps.
