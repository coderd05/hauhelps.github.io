Start a Branch

Follow the steps below to lead change in your community.

Step 1: Contact

Contact our Branch Coordinator, Maanya, at +1 508-395-0774.

Let her know your grade, your high school, and your intent to start a branch. She will guide you through the initial approval process and provide necessary materials.

Step 2: Propose

Reach out to your Vice Principal to express interest in starting a club. Use the template below to keep it professional:

Subject: Interest in Starting a New Club

Hello Mr./Ms. [Last Name],
My name is [Your Name]... I've recently become involved with Advocacy for the Unhoused, and I believe it would be a meaningful addition to our school...

The Pitch

Step 3: Organize

To become official, you'll need the following:

  • A core group of at least 5 people.
  • A Teacher Sponsor or advisor (can be from any department).

Congrats. You are now an official branch of our non-profit.
